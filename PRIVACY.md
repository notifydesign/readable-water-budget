# Privacy Policy, Readable Water Budget

Last updated: June 2026

Readable Water Budget does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal
information.

The extension runs entirely inside your own browser. When you open the
waterscope.us website while logged in, it reads your existing water-budget and
usage data from that page (using your own active session) and re-displays it in
a cleaner layout. That data never leaves your computer. It is not sent to us, to
any server, or to any third party. There are no analytics and no tracking.

The extension makes one outside request: it reads a small public text file from
GitHub (raw.githubusercontent.com) that lists the local irrigation shut-off
date, so that date can stay current without an app update. This is a read-only
request that sends none of your information. The looked-up date is cached in
your browser's local storage so it does not need to be downloaded on every
visit.

The extension runs only on https://waterscope.us and does nothing on any other
website.
