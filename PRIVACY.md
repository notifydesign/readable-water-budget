# Privacy Policy, Readable Water Budget

Last updated: June 2026

Readable Water Budget does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal
information.

The extension runs entirely inside your own browser. When you open the
waterscope.us website while logged in, it reads your existing water-budget and
usage data from that page (using your own active session) and re-displays it in
a cleaner layout. That data never leaves your computer. It is not sent to us, to
any server, or to any third party. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no
external network calls beyond the Waterscope site you are already using.

The extension only runs on https://waterscope.us and does nothing on any other
website.
